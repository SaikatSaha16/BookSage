ABOUT BOOK SAGE

Book sage is one of the world's leading independent academic and professional publishers. Founded 50 years ago by Sara Miller McCune, we remain majority owned by Sara who has ensured that the company will remain permanently independent. We are proud of our independence, which enables us to focus on the broad publishing needs of our communities, not simply to meet the needs of shareholders.
We publish over 800 textbooks, scholarly titles and handbooks a year across the social sciences, business and management, health sciences and humanities.
